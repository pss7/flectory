FLECTORY’s 5G CU/DU solution is based on the 3GPP standard, offering modularized Central Unit (CU) and Distributed Unit (DU) components for flexible network configurations and high-performance data processing.

It ensures seamless compatibility with Option 7.2 RUs without requiring an additional fronthaul gateway,and incorporates advanced noise cancellation technology to enhance uplink signal quality, delivering stable UL SNR.
