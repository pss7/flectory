PRIVATE 5G

Private 5G, also known as Local 5G, is an exclusive 5G network independently built and operated by a specific enterprise or institution. 

With ultra-high-speed data transmission, ultra-low latency, and the ability to connect a vast number of devices simultaneously, it delivers optimal network environments across various sites such as smart factories, logistics centers, hospitals, and campuses.
